About Me

I decided to join this program after Corona virus pandemic caused me to get furloughed from Penske Auto Motive Company. When I got furloughed it was an eye-opening experience for me, kind of showing me the career path I was on was not that stable. Ive always had interest in computers and how the work. I built my first PC myself when I was in middle school only getting help by watching YouTube videos. Ive been building my own computers and computers for friends and family ever since. A former co-worker of mine left and took a similar course and after talking to him while on furlough it really piqued my interest in a career switch. Which inevitable lead to me to submitting my two weeks' notice and signing up for this course. However, in my free time I spend lots of time with my cars. I spend almost all my free time with friends working on my cars or theirs or at race track breaking the cars were fixing.
